Our Message

At Narratify, we view technology through the lens of storytelling, developing products as if we were crafting narratives. This approach represents a uniquely Japanese way of engaging with technology, akin to the creative processes behind novels, films, and games.

Today, emerging technologies like AI, blockchain, and quantum computing are asking us a crucial question: "What kind of future will you choose?"

Our answer is not a world of arrogant control over everything. Instead, we strive for a new harmony between technology, nature, and humanity.

Narratify steps beyond conventional views of technology, exploring new concepts rooted in animatism—the Japanese perspective that sees life in all things. We aim to introduce the world to a new technological viewpoint based on "Vernacular Technology"—indigenous tech wisdom unique to Japan.

Through this, we contribute to realizing a future where humans, technology, and nature are united as one.
